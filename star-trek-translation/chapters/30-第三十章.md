# 第三十章

塔马斯和他的克林贡特遣队 huddled close together，他们的 backs 朝向一堵 half wall，但没有 touching 它。Looking over the wall 可以看到下一层，那里三个 generators 中的一个 rotating around 一个复杂的 crystalline structure。另外两个 generators 在类似的 compartments 中，以 triangular pattern 距离下面那个一百步远。Getting to their present location 已经 cost 两个凯尔文守卫 their lives。Fortunately for Tammas and Gowr，他们不是真正的凯尔文 form，而是 transformed 看起来像罗慕伦人。塔马斯没有 actual killing，但尽管如此，他的 poncho 上有 blood stains 来 show for it。Below，around the generator itself，有许多 Romulan guards。其中有一个 female，她 wearing 一个 bracelet，似乎是由与 generator 相同的 material 制成。塔马斯给了 strict warning，任何 wearing glowing bracelet 的人都不许被 killed，到目前为止，只有 women 被发现 wearing 它们。

"那里有七个，"恩伦特 counted。"但我很 puzzled。我从未听说过有不 carry disruptors 的罗慕伦守卫。"

"他们并不 exactly affiliated 与罗慕伦政府，"塔马斯 offered。

"我相信他们是凯尔文人，处于罗慕伦 form。"

"不过，恩伦特说得有道理。一个没有 disruptor 的罗慕伦人就像一个没有 slime 的丹尼比安 slime devil。我们没有看到任何人有任何 sort of energetic weapons。只有 swords and knives。如果他们是克林贡人我会 understand，但他们只是罗慕伦人，或者如果你愿意的话是凯尔文人，"高尔 said，spitting with disgust。

塔马斯将双手 brought together 在他的 mouth 前，仿佛 in prayer，同时他从 friends' observations 中 drew conclusions。"当然。那 makes sense。必须是如此。"

"Another feeling？"恩伦特 asked。

"是的，"塔马斯 said 带着 smile，rubbing his hands together。"这将比我 thought 的更容易。如果我是 right，我们甚至不需要 plant 我们的 explosives。"

"Explain，"高尔 asked。

"Obviously 这些 crystal generators，或者更 precisely 说，psychic accumulators，对 disruptor energy 很 sensitive。也许 energized crystal resonates 与 normal phaser blast 相同的 frequency。直接 phaser 或 disruptor 的 harmonics 会导致 crystal's lattice shatter and disintegrate。"

"你是说我们 shoot 其中一个 accumulators，整个 complex 就会完蛋？"高尔 asked。

"Exactly！"塔马斯 praised。

"那不会给我们 much time to evacuate，"恩伦特 said。

"哦，但会的！"塔马斯 assured her。"It will be like striking a tuning fork。The crystal will begin to vibrate，the vibrations will spread through the entire crystalline structure，spreading out from the impact source like ripples in a pond，然后 the echo will induce the overlapping harmonic and enharmonic dissonance，这将 shatter the entire crystal grid，当那发生时，sudden release of all that energy 应该会产生一些 rather dramatic results……"

"Sounds reasonable，"高尔 said，considering it a moment。他 nodded，as he talked himself into accepting Tam's theory。

On queue，他们三人 drew their disruptors，tuned them to the maximum power setting，counted to three，stood，turned，aimed down at the generator-accumulator，并 simultaneously fired。他们不可能 more synchronous，即使他们 rehearsed it a hundred times。他们 acted as if of one mind, and one spirit，sustaining fire 也许 several seconds too long。The unexpected results forced them to duck back down below the half wall。

disruptor bolts 确实 hit 了 accumulator。从那里，三条 separate energy bolts ricochet off the accumulator，分别 heading for the wall or floor，只是再次 ricochet off the lines of crystals embedded in the floor or wall，dividing it into multiple beams。每次 beams struck，它们 divided into two separate beams of energy 并 flew to other point in the room 重复这个过程。这是一个 extremely loud, hair raising 的过程。其中一条 bolt exited the chamber below 并在 bounced off the ceiling 后 ended its life 在高尔的 feet。地板甚至没有被 charred。

高尔和恩伦特 scowled at Tammas。他 shrugged，helplessly。

"再说一次，energized crystal 可能有 reflective properties，使得 disruptor fire extremely dangerous，甚至对 firing 的人，"塔马斯 offered。

高尔 growled，drew his bat'telh，并 jumped over the parapet 到下面的 floor。

"等等！"塔马斯 yelled，有点 late。他 dropped his disruptor 并 followed Gowr over the wall。只有在 falling 时他才 wish 他 had considered the situation more thoroughly。这比他 expected 的 drop 更长，而 stairs 就在他 behind。他 hit the floor 并 rolled，evenly enough distributed the energy from the fall 以避免 injury。他 came up standing。他刚 stood，就不得不 duck and roll 以避免一把 Romulan sword coming at him。他 fell over two dead Romulan guards，他们 neatly absorbed partial disruptor bolts，并 came up standing a second time，with his sword in a defensive posture。

恩伦特 had landed on the floor behind the Romulan attacking Tammas。罗慕伦人的 sword came down hard on Tam's sword，他对 block growled in frustration。他甚至似乎没有 notice 他已经被 N'lent slain。他只是 fell over 当她 withdrew her sword from his flesh。她 stepped out of its way 并 moved to attack another guard。塔马斯 stood，searching for someone to fight。高尔 was taking two on for himself，他们 were forcing him towards the corner。塔马斯 did the unexpected。他 slipped up behind one of the Romulans 并 administered a Vulcan Nerve pinch to the shoulder。守卫 fell to the floor，unconscious。高尔 dispatched the remaining opponent，然后 killed the guard Tammas had rendered unconscious。

"你为什么要那样做？"塔马斯 asked。

"这样他就不会 wake and sneak up behind me！"高尔 said。

"你在哪里 learned that Vulcan nerve pinch？"恩伦特 asked Tammas。

"实际上我不知道，"塔马斯 said，trying to figure out where that had come from。"我没有 been formally trained。"
